For those who don't know yet, U-M has deployed a new version of the Friend guest
account system.  The two largest additions to the system are the addition of
user- and web-app-initiated mass invites.  While the end-user interface is
available -- https://friend.weblogin.umich.edu/friend/invite/ -- we, the CoSign
team, would like to work closely with a couple (few?) units on campus to create
pilot XML-RPC-enabled Friend services.

If you are interested in adding this functionality to any of your cosign-enabled
web apps, please let us know as soon as possible!

Sending invitations to create guest accounts:
http://www.itd.umich.edu/itcsdocs/s4337/

http://www.umich.edu/~umweb/software/cosign/cosign-friend-spec.txt
