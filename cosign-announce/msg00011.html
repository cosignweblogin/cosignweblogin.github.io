It is with great pleasure that the cosign team announces the release of cosign-1.1.0. Special thanks to Jeff Cousineau for pointing out a very minor, but overlooked, change that we missed in 1.0.1. Apologies to Jim Zajkowski for not including his patch in this release, but I promise it will make it to the 1.2 release.

MD5(cosign-1.1.0.tar.gz)= 7315246f32665c8c5d5d47a62825ef23

	cgi: BasicAuth capabilities
	cgi: rename uniqname to login
	cgi: remove htputs()
	daemon: app to clean cookie and tix by arbitrary idle time
	daemon: re-work read_cookie() to work with monster
	daemon: use mtime instead of atime for cookie idle out
	filter: fixed last stray version symbol problem
	filter: PSU bug - you can have multiple service names get tix and such

All these changes are minor but we are setting the stage for friend's official release!
