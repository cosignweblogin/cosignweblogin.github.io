Cosign 1.6.1 released

  • To: cosign-announce@xxxxxxxxx
  • Subject: Cosign 1.6.1 released
  • From: kevin mcgowan <clunis@xxxxxxxxx>
  • Date: Wed, 12 May 2004 00:12:18 -0400
The Cosign Team is pleased to announce the availability of version 1.6.1 of cosign [ MD5 f0f1c3bead08b923623c7a5fc6148c4e ]. Changelog at http://www.umich.edu/~umweb/software/cosign/changelog.txt

Changes:

krbpath is now MAXPATHLEN to allow for longer paths
common: snprintf() to prevent buffer overruns in krbpath & krb4path
html: an extremely rudimentary example services directory is now part of the html distribution

Many thanks to Phil Pishioneri from Penn State University and Brett Lomas from the University of Auckland for their contributions to this release.

Kevin
