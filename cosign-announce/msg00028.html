Cosign Announce: BabyCraig v1.0

  • To: cosign-announce@xxxxxxxxx
  • Subject: Cosign Announce: BabyCraig v1.0
  • From: kevin mcgowan <clunis@xxxxxxxxx>
  • Date: Wed, 29 Jun 2005 15:25:40 -0400
The Cosign team is proud to announce the release early Monday morning of BabyCraig 1.0. This work is a collaboration between Wes and Johanna and the first such release by members of the Cosign team. Features of BabyCraig 1.0 include:

     o Non-specialized, biologicial, autonomous agent
     o Easily portable (6lbs 2oz, 19.25 inches)
     o Similar in design to over half the planet (female)
     o Adorable

We're all terribly proud of Wes and Jo's work and extend them the sincerest congratulations. Jarod and I will be taking a more active role in shepherding Cosign while they concentrate on overseeing and maintaining their new release during her first few months.

grinning uncontrollably,

Kevin
